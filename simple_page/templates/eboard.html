April Dottin-Carter '23
President

she/her
I believe that when people with strong, positive morals and diverse backgrounds come together to create the world’s future technologies, the impact of tech is able to reach more communities and push us in the right direction towards global equality. Midway through my first year at Amherst, I took the initiative in reviving Amherst’s Womxn in Computer Science Club in order to create a powerful and supported community for fellow students who are womxn interested in computer science.

Outside of school and WiCS, I enjoy cooking vegetarian dishes, binge-watching reality shows, and reading my daily horoscope (I’m a Taurus). As for my future, I am an aspiring frontend web developer, and I plan to permanently move out of America and onto the road in an intricate and beautiful tiny house.

Cheers to a bright future for WiCS!

Hi, my name is Ashley! I’m the event manager for WiCS and a sophomore math major from New Jersey. While I’ve only taken the intro CS courses at Amherst and self-studied a data structures course, I was inspired to join WiCS from having led a women-in-STEM club in my high school.
